1  Overview

TealKit is an on-device AI agent application published by TealKit (at.ls.gr.tealkit). TealKit does not operate any backend servers, does not create user accounts, and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or any analytics platform.

All configuration, conversation history, indexed documents, and API credentials you enter are stored exclusively on your device using the operating system's secure storage and local databases.

2  Data stored on your device

The following data exists only on your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • AI provider API keys — stored in the OS secure keychain / keystore
  • Task definitions & conversation history — stored in a local SQLite database
  • Indexed documents & websites — stored in a local DuckDB database
  • Email / IMAP / SMTP credentials — stored in the OS secure keychain
  • Location coordinates — you may optionally save GPS coordinates; these never leave your device
  • Output files — saved to a folder you choose on your device's storage
  • App preferences (theme, language, retention days) — stored in local shared preferences

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling TealKit.

5  Children's privacy

TealKit is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your country). We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6  Data security

All sensitive credentials (API keys, passwords) are stored using the platform's secure keychain (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). Conversation data and indexed content are stored in local databases that are accessible only to TealKit and protected by the OS app sandbox.

Because no data is transmitted to our servers, there is no server-side breach risk associated with your TealKit data.

7  Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app's functionality changes. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any revision. Continued use of TealKit after a change constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
